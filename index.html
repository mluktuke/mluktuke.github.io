Projects

In my senior project, during my Bachelors, I worked on researching SDNs and the issues that currently exist with its security. My team and I worked to build a network, attack it, and then mitigate the attack. We worked with a Cisco Catalyst 2960X series switch, Raspberry Pis, a Mac mini, and our personal laptops to act as clients and controllers connected via the ethernet ports of the switch. I also worked with a team to research different computer vision algorithms that exist in OpenCV and test their performance on a Raspberry Pi. We went on to present this project at the 8th Annual Student RSCA Conference.
